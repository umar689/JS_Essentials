# ISP (Internet Service Provider) Explained

## ISP kya hota hai?

ISP ka full form hota hai:

> Internet Service Provider

Ye wo company hoti hai jo hume internet provide karti hai.

Simple words me:

> ISP hume internet se connect karta hai.

---

# Real Life Example

Agar tumhare ghar me WiFi chal raha hai ya mobile data aa raha hai,  
to uske peeche koi ISP hota hai.

Examples:

- Jio
- Airtel
- BSNL
- ACT
- Excitel

---

# Internet ka Flow

```text
Your Device
     ↓
WiFi / Mobile Data
     ↓
ISP
     ↓
Internet
     ↓
Website / Server
```

# ISP kya kya provide karta hai?

## 1. Internet Connection

- Fiber
- Broadband
- Mobile Data
- Satellite Internet

---

## 2. IP Address

Har device ko internet par ek unique IP deta hai.

Example:

```text
192.168.x.x
```

---

## 3. DNS Service

Website names ko IP address me convert karta hai.

Example:

```text
google.com → 142.250.xx.xx
```

---

## 4. Email Services

Kuch ISPs email bhi provide karte hain.

Example:

```text
user@bsnl.in
```

---

# ISP kaam kaise karta hai?

ISP ke paas bahut bade servers aur networking systems hote hain.

Wo:
- cables
- routers
- switches
- fiber optics

ke through users ko internet se connect karta hai.

---

## 1. Fiber Internet

Example:
- Jio Fiber

---

## 2. Broadband

---

## 3. Mobile Network

4G / 5G internet.

---

## 4. Satellite Internet

- Starlink

---

# Difference Between ISP and WiFi

| ISP | WiFi |
|---|---|
| Internet provide karta hai | Internet ko wireless banata hai |
| Example: Jio, Airtel | Example: Router |
| Internet source | Internet sharing method |
